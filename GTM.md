# Go-To-Market — SpendPilot AI Audit

## Target User (Primary)

**Engineering Manager or CTO at a 5–50 person startup**

- Pays for 3–8 AI tools across their team
- Has no formal process for reviewing AI subscriptions
- Feels vague guilt about the monthly AI bill but hasn't audited it
- Responds to concrete dollar figures, not abstract "optimization" language

**Secondary:** Freelance developers and solo founders who stack multiple AI coding tools.

---

## The Core Insight

Most teams don't overspend on AI tools because they're reckless. They overspend because:
1. Someone signed up for a trial that auto-converted to paid
2. The team grew past the threshold where a cheaper plan made sense
3. Two people independently subscribed to overlapping tools

SpendPilot surfaces these patterns in 3 minutes with zero setup.

---

## Acquisition Strategy — First 100 Users

### Channel 1: Developer Communities (highest intent)
- Post in **r/ExperiencedDevs**, **r/startups**, **r/SideProject** with a genuine "I built this" post showing a real audit result screenshot
- Share in **Hacker News Show HN** — the "free tool that audits your AI spend" angle is highly relevant to the HN audience
- Post in **Dev.to** and **Hashnode** with a technical writeup on how the audit engine works

### Channel 2: Twitter/X Developer Audience
- Tweet a thread: "I audited my AI tool stack and found $X/month in waste. Here's what I found and how to check yours."
- Tag relevant accounts: @levelsio, @marc_louvion, @IndieHackers
- The shareable report link is the viral mechanic — every shared report is a referral

### Channel 3: Slack/Discord Communities
- Post in **Indie Hackers Slack**, **YC Startup School Discord**, **Buildspace Discord**
- Frame as: "Built a free tool for founders — would love feedback"

### Channel 4: Cold Outreach (10 targeted)
- Identify 10 CTOs at Series A startups via LinkedIn
- Send a personalized audit of their publicly-known stack (based on job postings mentioning tools)
- Offer to run a free audit call

---

## Unfair Distribution Advantages

1. **The shareable report is the product** — every user who shares their report creates a branded link that drives inbound traffic
2. **The audit is free** — zero friction to try, which maximizes top-of-funnel conversion
3. **The result is a dollar figure** — "$684/month in savings" is inherently shareable and screenshot-worthy
4. **Timing** — AI tool spend is a new and growing pain point with no established solution
